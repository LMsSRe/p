About Me

Hello! I'm Sujoy Roy, a passionate and certified cybersecurity professional with over 7 years of experience in defending digital fortresses. Based in Bangladesh, I specialize in ethical hacking and penetration testing, helping organizations identify and mitigate critical vulnerabilities before malicious actors can exploit them.

My journey in cybersecurity is driven by a relentless curiosity and a commitment to staying ahead of the evolving threat landscape. I hold industry-recognized certifications like CEH, OSCP, and CompTIA Security+, which validate my expertise in both offensive and defensive security practices.
